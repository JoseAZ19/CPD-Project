About Us

Clarke’s Personal Development (CPD) provides elite soccer development to beginner athletes, who require more personalized guidance on correcting technical mistakes. CPD’s goal is to have each athlete develop confidence in their individual skills and value their abilities. Emphasis is placed on defensive and dribbling techniques which gives our young players the ability to hone their skills. CPD also provides conversational Spanish tutoring, with experienced Spanish tutors. in order to assist with any potential language barriers preventing anyone from achieving their ultimate goal.
